About

What is Fake News?

  • Fake news is news designed to spread hoaxes, propaganda and misinformarion deliberately.
  • It denotes a type of yellow journalism that intentionally presents misinformation or hoaxes spreading through traditional print news media and recent online media.
  • This is often done to impose specific ideas and is usually achieved with political agenda.
  • Fake news mimic real headlines and twist the story.

Why tracking is IMPORTANT?

  • Modern life has become quite suitable and the people of the world have to thank the vast contribution of internet technology for transmission and information sharing.
  • This is an evolution in human history but at the same time unfocused the line between real media and maliciously forged media.
  • Today anyone can publish content, credible or not and can consume that. Sadly, fake news accumulates a great deal of attention over the internet, especially on social media.
